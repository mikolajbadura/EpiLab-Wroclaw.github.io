Explore Our Research

Research Focus

Quantum Photonics & Emitters

Development of deterministically fabricated, bright, and Purcell-enhanced InAs/InP quantum dots-based nonclassical photon sources for quantum communication in the C-band. We also explore integrated quantum photonics on hybrid InP/SiO2 platforms.

Advanced Semiconductor Lasers

Pushing the boundaries of semiconductor laser technology. Our research covers broken symmetry VCSELs to increase efficiency, surface-emitting lasers with MHCG subwavelength diffraction gratings, and single-mode cascade lasers for optical gas detection.

Epitaxy & Nanofabrication

Growth of advanced A-III-N and A-III-B-V heterostructures using MOVPE and HVPE techniques. We specialize in the complete technological process: from crystal growth and nano-lithography to advanced structural and optical characterization.

Facilities & Equipment

Our laboratories include 500 m² of highly controlled clean rooms (class < 10 000) equipped with a complete technological line.

Epitaxy Systems

MOVPE (AIXTRON CCS 3x2”, AIX200 R&D) and HVPE reactors for complex heterostructures and thick GaN layers growth with precise n/p doping control.

Plasma & Lithography

Oxford Instruments PlasmaLab for RIE, PECVD, and ICP-PECVD. UV Photolithography (Karl Suss MA56) and full mask fabrication capabilities.

Advanced Characterization

AFM/STM (Veeco MultiMode), SEM (Hitachi Su6600 w/ EDX), high-frequency HEMT measurement setup, OBIC, and UV u-PL mapping.
