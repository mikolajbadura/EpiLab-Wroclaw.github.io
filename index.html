Epitaxy of Photonic Structures Laboratory

Wrocław University of Science and Technology, Faculty of Electronics, Photonics and Microsystems

Pioneering research in quantum photonics, advanced semiconductor lasers (VCSELs, cascades), and the epitaxy of highly efficient nanostructures for next-generation optoelectronics.

Explore Our Research

Research Focus

Quantum Photonics & Emitters

Development of deterministically fabricated, bright, and Purcell-enhanced InAs/InP quantum dots-based nonclassical photon sources for quantum communication in the C-band. We also explore integrated quantum photonics on hybrid InP/SiO2 platforms.

Advanced Semiconductor Lasers

Pushing the boundaries of semiconductor laser technology. Our research covers broken symmetry VCSELs to increase efficiency, surface-emitting lasers with MHCG subwavelength diffraction gratings, and single-mode cascade lasers for optical gas detection.

Epitaxy & Nanofabrication

Growth of advanced A-III-N and A-III-B-V heterostructures using MOVPE and HVPE techniques. We specialize in the complete technological process: from crystal growth and nano-lithography to advanced structural and optical characterization.

Facilities & Equipment

Our laboratories include 500 m² of highly controlled clean rooms (class < 10 000, with class 100 nano-lithography zones) equipped with a complete technological line.

Epitaxy Systems

MOVPE (AIXTRON CCS 3x2” for A-III-N, AIX200 R&D for A-III-B-V) and HVPE reactors for complex heterostructures and thick GaN layers growth with precise n/p doping control.

Plasma & Lithography

Oxford Instruments PlasmaLab 80plus/100 for RIE, PECVD, and ICP-PECVD. UV Photolithography (Karl Suss MA56, > 0.6 µm) and full mask fabrication capabilities.

Advanced Characterization

AFM/STM (Veeco MultiMode), SEM (Hitachi Su6600 w/ EDX), high-frequency HEMT measurement setup (up to 20 GHz), OBIC, UV u-PL mapping, and DLTS systems.
